# Acoustyx

ACOUSTYX is a group event, with acoustic fusion music as the genre. All music played must be purely acoustic and must have a blend of Indian and Western Music. The competition is centered around semi-professional musicians. (3 piece drum kit?)

## Rules

* Online registration: Teams shall be required to upload a video which should have at least 5 members of the group in it. Selection shall be at the sole discretion of Snuphoria.
* Teams of 5-8 people are allowed, preferably a good mix of instrumentalists and singers, both Indian and Western.
* 1st level will be a vocal solo (Indian and Western separately) with 1 accompanist allowed: 3+1 mins.
* 2nd level is Instrumental (solo or group): 5+2 mins.
* Each team starts with 2 lives and if they end up in the bottom, they lose a life.
* Points from all previous rounds shall be added up while selecting teams for the finale.
* Finale - 8+2 mins. All members of the team (instrumentalists + vocalists) are expected to be on stage
