## Rules


* Each tie would consist of three matches, First singles, Doubles, Reverse Singles, in that order.
* Each match would be a best of 3 sets (15 points each) during pool stage and 3 sets (21 points each) during semi-finals and finals.
* Time-out of 90 seconds is provided between each set. 
* Referee’s decision would be final and binding. 

Service

* The shuttle must be hit below the server's waist with the racquet head below the server's hand, and the server must have part of both feet stationary in contact with the ground.
* All BWF Rules would be followed.
